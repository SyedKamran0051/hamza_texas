About the Houston area Wienerschnitzel group

What matters to us

Quality Ingredients

We use only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients to craft our 100% all-beef hot dogs, sausages, and other menu items. From the spices to the toppings, quality is our priority.

Community Connection

We aim to be strong community partners wherever our restaurants are located by supporting local schools, sports teams, and nonprofits. We want to make a positive local impact.

Customer Satisfaction

Providing an enjoyable, quick, and affordable experience for every customer drives our team. We work hard to make sure each guest leaves satisfied by what they ordered.

Teamwork

Collaboration, open communication, and team spirit are central to how we operate. When our team works cohesively together with a shared purpose, we best serve our guests.

Innovation

By continually developing new menu items, promotions, and enhancements to the dining experience, we stay fresh while still delivering the classics people expect from us.

Value

Delivering a lot of quality food at affordable prices provides value to our customers. From combo meals to coupons to special discounts, value goes hand-in-hand with quality.
